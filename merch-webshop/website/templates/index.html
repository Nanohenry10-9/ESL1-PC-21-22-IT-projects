You can order school hoodies, sweaters, and totebags from the Pupils' Committee here.

Instructions

Do not reload or close this page until you have finished and confirmed your order, as it will not be saved otherwise.

There are three expandable categories in each order, one for each item. Click the category to open it, click it again to close it. When you are happy with the items in a category, leave it open, and it will be included in the order. If you don't want some category to be included, leave it closed.

In the hoodie and sweater categories, you can cycle between the size and colour options for each item by clicking the respective options. You can also have more than one of the same item in one order by clicking the Add another hoodie or Add another sweater button and then specifying the options for the new item. In the totebag category, you can only specify the quantity, as only one colour is available.

You can also have multiple orders by clicking the Add another order button. This is useful if you want to order for more than one person, but want to pay for all orders at once.

Once you are finished with the order, enter your email address at the bottom of the page, and press Confirm order. After that, an email will be sent with further instructions on how you can pay and ensure that your order gets processed. Note that your email address will be retained until the order has been collected.

Prices are indicated on each category below. Ordering a full set of all three items only costs 50 €.

You can view the designs, colour options, and size guides on a post on the PC's website.
